## Description (max 4000 characters)

Don't sign another contract without checking it first.

ContractShield is your AI-powered contract scanner that detects red flags, hidden risks, and unfair terms in seconds. Whether you're reviewing a lease, freelance agreement, employment offer, or business deal, ContractShield helps you understand what you're really agreeing to before you sign.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Scan or Upload - Use your camera to scan a paper document or import a PDF from your photo library
2. Instant Analysis - Our rule-based engine scans every clause for 20+ types of red flags including hidden fees, auto-renewal traps, vague liability terms, and one-sided obligations
3. Risk Score - Get a clear risk rating from Low to Critical so you know at a glance whether a contract is safe to sign
4. Detailed Breakdown - Each red flag is explained in plain English with the exact clause highlighted, so you know what's wrong and where to find it

WHAT CONTRACTSHIELD DETECTS

- Auto-renewal and evergreen clauses
- Hidden fees and penalty escalations
- One-sided termination rights
- Vague or ambiguous language
- Unlimited liability exposure
- Unfair indemnification requirements
- Overly broad non-compete restrictions
- Missing essential contract provisions
- Inconsistent terms and contradictions
- Unreasonable payment terms

AI-POWERED ANALYSIS (PRO)

Upgrade to Pro and unlock advanced AI analysis powered by cutting-edge language models. Get deeper insights, contextual explanations, negotiation suggestions, and plain-English summaries of complex legal language.

BUILT FOR REAL LIFE

- Renting an apartment? Scan the lease before you sign
- Starting a new job? Review your employment agreement
- Hiring a freelancer? Check the service contract
- Buying a car? Understand the sale agreement
- Running a business? Analyze NDAs and vendor contracts

YOUR DATA STAYS PRIVATE

ContractShield processes documents on-device whenever possible. Your contracts are never stored on our servers. Local analysis runs entirely on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's built-in text recognition technology.

KEY FEATURES

- Camera scanning with real-time OCR
- PDF import from photo library
- Instant red flag detection
- Color-coded risk levels (Critical, Warning, Standard)
- Detailed flag explanations in plain English
- Full scan history with search
- Pro tier with AI-powered deep analysis
- Dark mode support
- Designed for iPhone and iPad
