1. Introduction

ContractShield ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using ContractShield, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Name and email address when you contact support
  • Feedback messages you submit through the Contact Support feature

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Contract text you scan or import for analysis purposes
  • Device information (device type, operating system version) for compatibility
  • App usage data for improving our services

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We do not collect biometric data
  • We do not track your location

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide contract analysis and red flag detection services
  • To respond to your support inquiries and feedback
  • To process and manage your subscription (handled by Apple through the App Store)
  • To improve our analysis engine and app functionality
  • To communicate important updates about the service

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Your contract analysis history is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data unless you explicitly share it with us.

AI Analysis: When you use the Pro AI analysis feature, contract text is sent securely to our AI processing service. The text is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers after analysis is complete.

Feedback Data: Information submitted through Contact Support is transmitted securely and stored for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) to protect your data.

5. Third-Party Services

ContractShield uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit: For subscription management and payment processing. Apple's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • AI Analysis Service: For Pro-tier contract analysis. Text is processed in real-time and not retained.

We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics services that track you across apps and websites.

8. Data Retention
