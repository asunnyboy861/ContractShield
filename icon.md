# ContractShield App Icon Prompt

A flat iOS app icon featuring a stylized shield with a checkmark, representing contract protection and security. The shield is centered on a deep navy blue gradient background, with the checkmark in crisp white. The design follows Apple's minimalist aesthetic with clean geometric shapes, no text, no 3D effects, and no shadows. The shield has slightly rounded edges and a modern, professional look. The color palette uses navy blue (#1a365d) as the primary background with a subtle gradient to a lighter blue (#2b6cb0), and pure white (#ffffff) for the shield and checkmark. The icon should feel trustworthy, professional, and premium - suitable for a legal/financial tool in the US market. Flat design, iOS standard, 1024x1024, no rounded corners (iOS applies them automatically).
